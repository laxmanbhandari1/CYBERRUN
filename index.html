ABOUT THE GAME

CyberDefend Runner

A 3D cyberpunk endless runner that teaches real-world cybersecurity — through gameplay, not instruction.

CyberDefend Runner places players in a 3D cyberpunk city overrun by digital threats. As you sprint through neon-lit streets, real cyber threats — HACKERS, PHISHING bots, RANSOMWARE — physically emerge and chase your network nodes.

Every encounter is an education moment disguised as gameplay. Dodge a PHISHING bot and BYTE (your AI companion) explains what phishing is in 2 seconds — while the game never pauses.

The leaderboard is real-time and global, powered by Supabase. Only your best score counts. One row per player. Compete as yourself or go incognito as a Guest.

Built by a team of four university students for the HCI & App Development 2026 module — using Godot 4, Blender, and Supabase.
